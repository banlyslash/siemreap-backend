# Appendix

## A. Glossary of Terms

- **Leave Request**: Formal application for time off
- **Leave Balance**: Remaining available leave days by category
- **Approval Chain**: Sequence of approvers for a request
- **Leave Type**: Category of leave (e.g., Annual, Sick, Personal)
- **Half-Day Leave**: Leave that covers only half of a working day
- **Working Days**: Business days excluding weekends and holidays
- **Leave Allocation**: Number of leave days assigned to an employee
- **Leave Entitlement**: Leave days an employee is eligible for
- **Leave Year**: The annual period for leave calculation (calendar or fiscal)
- **Carry-over**: Unused leave days transferred to the next leave year
